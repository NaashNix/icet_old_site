Unleashing Creativity: iCET Partners with Leo Club of University of Moratuwa for Dev{Thon} 1.0

iCET is proud to announce its partnership with the Leo Club of University of Moratuwa for the highly anticipated Dev{Thon} 1.0 competition. Dev{Thon} 1.0 is a dynamic web development competition designed to challenge and inspire aspiring developers across Sri Lanka to showcase their skills and creativity in full-stack web development.

The competition, organized by the Leo Club of University of Moratuwa, aims to promote innovation and creativity in the field of web development while providing a platform for students to showcase their talents and learn from their peers. With every line of code, participants have the opportunity to become better developers and make their mark in the industry.

Website development is a valuable skill for undergraduates to learn, offering them the chance to develop professional skills that will benefit them in their future careers. By participating in Dev{Thon} 1.0, students not only enhance their digital skills but also build their portfolios, establish personal brands, and network with potential employers and collaborators.

iCET's partnership with the Leo Club of University of Moratuwa as a platinum sponsor underscores its commitment to nurturing young entrepreneurial talent in Sri Lanka. By supporting initiatives like Dev{Thon} 1.0, iCET aims to empower the next generation of developers and foster innovation in the tech industry.

Dev{Thon} 1.0 concluded successfully on April 29, 2024, marking a milestone in the journey of young undergraduate entrepreneurs in Sri Lanka. As the competition draws to a close, participants can take pride in their achievements and look forward to the exciting opportunities that lie ahead in their web development careers.

Interactive Viva Sessions

At ICET, we believe in providing our students with a holistic and immersive learning experience that goes beyond traditional classroom instruction. As part of our commitment to excellence, we conduct Viva sessions periodically.

These Viva sessions play a vital role in the educational journey of our students, allowing them to demonstrate their knowledge and practical skills to a panel of expert lecturers and industry professionals.

Unlike written exams, which primarily test memorization and recall, Viva sessions assess a student's depth of understanding, critical thinking skills, and ability to apply theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios.

In our programs, Viva sessions serve as a platform for students to showcase their knowledge and practical know-how acquired throughout the course. Whether it's discussing coding techniques, explaining algorithmic concepts, or presenting project prototypes, Viva sessions allow students to demonstrate their mastery of the material and receive constructive feedback from our expert panel.

By providing a platform for students to demonstrate their knowledge, receive expert feedback, and engage with industry professionals, these sessions play a crucial role in preparing our students for the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead in their software development careers.

iCD - iCET Certified Developer

iCD – iCET Certified Developer is a comprehensive program that is designed to prepare individuals for a professional career in the IT Software Industry. The course is targeted towards IT graduates, non-IT graduates or professionals and anyone who is currently employed and looking to start or improve their career in the IT Software Industry. iCD is available in two modes, online and physical learning. The online option allows students to complete the program virtually under the guidance of a panel of esteemed lecturers. The physical learning option offers students the opportunity to learn and interact with lecturers and peers in our sophisticated labs. Upon completion of the iCD certification, students will be equipped with analytical skills, design skills and the ability to implement practical self-projects resulting in them being 100% industry ready. If you’re looking to start or improve your career in the IT Software Industry, the iCD - iCET Certified Developer Program is the best choice for you
